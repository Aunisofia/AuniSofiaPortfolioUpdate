Here The Story

Leadership Camp | 22.9.2025 - 24.9.2025

Had the opportunity to attend the leadership camp alongside all crew members from main crew to college crew. It was trully one of the most thrilling experience I've ever had that filled with unforgettable activities like swimming, "mandi lumpur", singing together and adventurous night programs.

Beyond the fun, I was grateful to meet so many people and build meaningful connections. This camp taught me valuable lessons in courage, time management and how to communicate effectively with my team members.

Preparation | 25.9.2025 - 27.9.2025

In the three days leading up to the event, we prepared by setting camera, learned basics of shooting video and figured out how to edit using the DaVinci Resolve. We really made the most of those days to ensure everything went smoothly and we could avoid any last-minutes problem or any technical issues.

We also shoot a montage video to be shown to the students on their last day of 3M.

Minggu Mesra Mahasiswa 2025/2026 | 28.9.2025 - 1.10.2025

The four day event was incredibly tiring but it was absolutely the ultimate experience I've ever had. Never in my mind did I imagine that I would become someone who would welcome thounsands of new freshman into the university.

We had a schedule made by our Head of Videography Unit to ensure we covered all locations. Since the main hall was undergoing renovation, the events were held in separate places around the university and the students were places in several different halls.

Apart from that, we also divided our work where some of us went to film the video, while the others stayed in the club room to edit. We were responsible for creating a daily recap to show the students a summary of what they did that day.

On the final day, we had sports day in the morning where all freshman students competed between their residential colleges. This was the moment for the freshman to show their atheletic talents and potentially qualify to represent the university in the future tournaments.

At night, we had Malam Kebudayaan (Cultural Night) where we got to see costume parade by each college, skit competitions and sing-along session together at Studium Azman Hashim. It was a great program and I hope to have the availability to join this kind of program again.

What I learned:

1. Team working and manangement
2. Time management
3. Video filming
4. Video Editing (DaVinci Resolve)
