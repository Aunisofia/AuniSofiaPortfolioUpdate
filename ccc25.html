Here The Story

CCC '25 is a program designed to help the first year students sharpen their C++ programming skills with the guidance from the senior students proficient in the language.

Though it was a small scale program, the experience was incredibly meaningful for me. I had the opportunity to work closely with my team members to find sources and prepare gifts for the winners of the Quizizz activities. It was rewarding to contribute to an initiative that supports junior students while celebrating their achievements.
